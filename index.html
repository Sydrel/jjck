About Us

Your Home for Cards & Community

Welcome to JJCK Collectibles, the Treasure Coast's premier family-owned sports card and collectibles shop! We specialize in a wide range of trading cards and collectibles, including Topps, Panini, Pokémon, One Piece, Magic: The Gathering, Lorcana, and more.

Located in a welcoming, family-friendly environment, our knowledgeable team is here to help beginners and seasoned collectors alike find the perfect additions to their collections.

  • Sports Cards
  • Pokémon
  • One Piece
  • Magic & Lorcana
  • Rare Memorabilia
  • Trading Cards

At JJCK Collectibles, we're more than just a shop — we're a community built on a shared love for collecting.
